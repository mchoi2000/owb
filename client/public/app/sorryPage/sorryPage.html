Our apologies!

Before you can register for Provider Workbench, you must have an approved offering that is associated with your IBM id.

What you should do:

  • If you received an invitation to publish your offering on IBM Marketplace using Provider Workbench, sign in with the IBM id you used to qualify that offering. Contact pwbteam@us.ibm.com if you still need help.
  • If you submitted an offering to IBM for qualification, wait for an approval email from the IBM review team. If it has been more than two business days since you submitted your offering for qualification, contact joinnow@us.ibm.com.
  • If you have not yet submitted an offering for Marketplace qualification, submit a qualification form for approval.
